Juan Uribe

Being weird is the side effect of awesomeness. The bags under my eyes are Gucci. I deserve a medal every day I don’t stab someone with a fork. I told the doctor that I’d broken my arm in several places. He said not to go to those places.

Professional procrastinator. I was at a funeral and the widow asked if I would say a word. I said “plethora”. She said “thanks, that means a lot.” The best things in life are not things. The truth will set you free, but first it will piss you off.
